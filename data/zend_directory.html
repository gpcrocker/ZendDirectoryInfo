Zend Certified Engineer

Looking for a Zend Certified Engineer (ZCE) in your area to work with? Trying to verifiy the certification of a Developer?  The Zend Certified Engineer Directory (formally the Zend Yellow Pages) was created to help you find the developer you are looking for.  You can search by ZCE name, country, company name, certification type or their ClientCandidateID number.  New ZCEs are added every day.  Click on the map to see the ZCEs in your country!
